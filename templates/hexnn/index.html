At low K (4) prototypes pack the lattice and the system behaves like the positional K=4 family. At high K (64, 256) the prototypes become a sparse Voronoi partition — most random rules look like noise; class IV regions are rarer but visually closer to organic gradients. The "New rule" button generates a fresh random genome at the current K; "Reseed grid" randomises initial cell colours without touching the rule.
