Concept Vocabulary

A toy inventory of semantic concepts — not words in any particular language. Each concept is an idea, not a lexical item, so it's neither declined nor conjugated. Four nested tiers: the minimum ten, a Swadesh-scale core, the full ~700-concept dictionary, and a broad 10K-ish lexicon extending it with named taxa and cultural specifics.
