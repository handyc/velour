signs

A general-purpose corpus of lexical signs across human signed languages. Each Sign is one (Lemma × Variety) realisation: a sequence of frames, each frame a 30-cylinder hand pose (10 fingers × 3 phalanges) plus optional OpenPose-derived body context. Hand pose stored in the same form the in-Velour viewer consumes natively.
