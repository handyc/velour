Mutation flips a fraction of the seed's 16,384 lookup entries to a uniformly-random colour; lower rates produce closer cousins.

Mutation rate
0.001 ≈ 16 entries flipped (barely distinguishable from parent) · 0.01 ≈ 164 (visibly different but obviously related) · 0.05 ≈ 819 (more distant cousins) · 0.1 ≈ 1638 (drift toward chaos)
