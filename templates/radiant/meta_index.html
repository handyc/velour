Meta-tournaments

A meta-tournament runs several tournaments, each with the fitness weights jittered by ± a configurable fraction, and promotes the best champion across those runs as the meta-champion. The point is to test how stable a winning bundle is across reasonable variation in what we actually care about — TCO vs. headroom vs. isolation.
