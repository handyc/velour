Default-Mode loop · live

No prompt, no input. The substrate is one CA grid; every tick it steps forward, four tokens are read by hashing row-groups, those tokens go through caformer's nano_gpt (the L2 recursive composition: 3 transformer blocks of CAs), the argmax of the output logits is sampled, and the sampled token is XOR-folded back into the substrate. That fold-back is the loop closure — without it the substrate just iterates its own attractor; with it the nano_gpt is reading its own past output and steering the substrate toward whatever its rules find probable.
