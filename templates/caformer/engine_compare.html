how to read the panels

  • Match count: how many bytes of the produced output exactly equal the trained pair's expected. N/N = full match (the model "knows" the answer); 0/N = nothing matched (untrained or budget-out).
  • Wall ms: end-to-end inference latency. board128 with ?tier=auto is typically ~5-50 ms; cell8+256 is ~200-1000 ms (4× larger LUT × 4× cells × 2× ticks).
  • Speed ratio: board128 / cell8 wall. A value of e.g. "0.05×" means board128 is 20× faster than cell8 on this prompt.
