How it works

  • Pick one of mandelbrot / julia / burning_ship / tricorn / multibrot / newton / phoenix at random
  • Generate the K=4 LUT (16,384 bytes) for a random region
  • Run a fast class-4 probe: 12 ticks on a 32×32 random state, 4-tick transient, measure mean cells-changed-per-tick
  • Accept iff activity ∈ [0.02, 0.55] (the "edge of chaos" band)
  • Save as .lut + .png in the pool
  • When the pool exceeds --max-pool, oldest dreams get culled

Dreams from this pool can be loaded into /caformer/lutview/ to play with interactively, or composed into /caformer/quarter-compose/ as cell8 personalities, or piped into the FTV's channel pool via caformer_emit_tv --bake-corpus N once we wire it to the dreams dir.
