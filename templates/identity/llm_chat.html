LLM chat

a tiny terminal for consulting an outside voice · results can be ingested as IdentityAssertions

Identity can query an external LLM and ingest the response as part of its structured self-record. The LLM acts as a foreign observer of Velour — whatever it says is a claim from an outside voice that the operator can promote into Velour's Identity Document under the documentary frame.
