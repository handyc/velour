evolve head ensembles

A head is one textmask transform — (mode, table, component, generation). An ensemble is K heads applied together to one input; outputs stack and concatenate. The GA evolves a population of M ensembles by tournament selection + crossover + per-head mutation. Default fitness rewards lexical diversity across the stacked outputs (cheap, no LLM). The next phase swaps in an LLM perplexity scorer.
