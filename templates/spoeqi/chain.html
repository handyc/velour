chain

Run text through a sequence of mask stages. Stage K's output becomes stage K+1's input. Each stage picks its own mode (char or token), mapping, component and generation; leave a stage's mode blank to skip it. The final output text is the result of all configured stages applied in order — the natural input to the oracle or LoRA pipeline.
