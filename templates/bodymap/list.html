Bodymap

Wearable biometric mesh. Each ESP32-S3 SuperMini sits on a body segment (torso, forearm, upper leg…) and reports motion coherence with its peers. The firmware clusters those observations into a topology and posts its assigned role here. Operators can override any role via the admin — the server will then ignore further firmware assignments for that node.
