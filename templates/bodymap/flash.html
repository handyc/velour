Flash a bodymap node

Plug an ESP32-S3 SuperMini into USB, pick the device below, click Flash. The server pushes the three pre-built binaries (bootloader.bin / partitions.bin / firmware.bin) through esptool.py over serial — no PlatformIO compile step on click, so a re-flash is a few seconds. If the firmware tree ever gets out of date, click Rebuild first.
