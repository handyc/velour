Import from another app

Pull a CA recipe from elsewhere in Velour: a class-4 rule from taxon, a TrainedModel's embed rule from caformer, a Mandelbrot walk's seed from loupe, a spoeqi component, an escher motif. The hex K=4 rule + the init seed together form the recipe; every adapter falls back to a class-4 random rule when its source app isn't installed.
