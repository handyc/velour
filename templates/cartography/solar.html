Solar System

The Solar System view is the trickiest scale to get right. v1 of cartography intentionally leaves it as a placeholder rather than rendering a half-finished 2D toy view. Either of the following external viewers will give you what you want until v2:

NASA Eyes on the Solar System Heavens-Above Solar System TheSkyLive 3D

v2 will use skyfield (already in requirements after chronos Phase 2c) to compute live planet positions and render a 2D top-down view with selectable date.
