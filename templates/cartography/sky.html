Sky · Galaxy · Universe

Sky-survey viewers are large JavaScript bundles (Aladin Lite is ~2MB) and the in-browser experience is fragile. Velour's approach for v1: link to the excellent external viewers and let them do the heavy work. They render instantly and they keep their data updated independently of Velour.

Aladin Lite (CDS Strasbourg) Sky-Map.org WorldWide Telescope Stellarium Web In-The-Sky Skymap

Aladin Lite is the most powerful (HiPS tile layers across many wavelengths). Stellarium Web is the friendliest if you just want to know what's overhead right now. Sky-Map.org and WorldWide Telescope cover the middle ground.
