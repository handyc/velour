Evolution-Engine-style competitions between rendered Zoetrope reels. Each tournament picks a random sample, scores them under a chosen fitness mode, and crowns the best one. Components: density (frames per second of the cut), sonority (speech samples × volume), entropy (color richness of the first frame), size_per_s (MB/second). Aesthetic is a weighted blend with random noise so repeats don't always pick the same reel.
