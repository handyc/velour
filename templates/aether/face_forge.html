How it works

Click any face to breed 9 new mutations from it. The parent sits in the center (highlighted). Save stores the genome — use saved faces as avatars in future worlds. Every face is drawn from ~20 stacked 2D layers at 60fps, with a per-face L-system animation program: symbols like b (blink), S (smile), L/R (look), W (wink), P/p (pupil), E (brow raise), H (head tilt) expanded through each face's own production rules.
