Notebook templates

Pre-built Jupyter notebooks intended to run on Open OnDemand hosts (ALICE in particular). A notebook here is effectively a kiosk-style GUI for a workflow — ipywidgets buttons and dropdowns on top, the underlying Slurm / container calls tucked into collapsed cells. A future Conduit target of kind slurm_ondemand will auto-open these on the cluster; for now, download the .ipynb and upload it into your ALICE OnDemand Jupyter session.
