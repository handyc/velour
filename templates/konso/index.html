Konso — syntactic trees

Konso (autonym Af Xonso) is a Lowland East Cushitic language of southwest Ethiopia with ~250,000 speakers. Word order is SOV, phrases are postpositional, relative clauses follow their head noun, and case is marked by clitics rather than suffixes. Trees here are written in Chomsky-style labelled-bracket notation ([S [NP nama] [VP [NP tika] [V gupe]]]) and rendered as inline SVG.
