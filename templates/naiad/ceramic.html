Notes

Particles spawn at the top edge each tick at the chosen flow rate (a fraction "contam %" of them are red contaminants, the rest are blue water). Particles that reach the bottom edge are removed from the count and tallied as passed through. Contaminant cells whose 6 hex neighbours contain ≥3 ceramic walls become trapped and stop moving — that's how the filter cleans water. Higher contam-trapped / contam-thru ratio = better filter.
